Courses

Prevention is the best way to keep your home pest-free. Unfortunately, it isn't always fool-proof, and pests might still find ways to sneak in. We're here to give you the knowledge to tackle even the toughest jobs.

  • Going Green Pest Control 101

    The beginner's guide to going green. If you're brand new to green methods, or haven't treated pests by yourself, this is the place to start.

  • How to Practice Green Pest Control to Save You Time And Money

    Take pest control into your own hands. Control the substances allowed in and around your home. Know you're keeping your family healthy and safe. Save precious time and money to spend on the things you love instead of an expensive pest control company.

  • How to Use Common Household Items to Rid Your Home of Pests

    Don't believe you can use items you most likely already own to start preventing and taking care of pests in your life? Think again!

  • Advanced Green Home Pest Control Methods

    Been in the green game awhile and have what it takes to bring your game to the next level, but don't know the most effective methods and products? This is the course for you.

  • How to Coexist with Beneficial Pests

    No one likes to think of pests as beneficial, but the truth is that they are often vital to the global ecosystems. Looking at you, bees. That said, no one wants to host them in their homes.

  • Check for Hitchhiking Pests at Hotels

    Don't bring home unwanted hitchhikers after a vacation or business trip. Learn how to check for pests before making yourself comfortable in your temporary home while travelling.
