About Abate-A-Pest

At Abate-A-Pest, we're committed to taking a greener, more natural approach at life. Nobody likes pests. That's why they're so aptly named pests. We believe with a bit of effort we can live in harmony with them while minimizing the use of harsh, enviromentally damaging chemicals, and we can all thrive in our own spaces.

Who Are We?

Ralph Swenson

Owner &
Licensed Operator

Ralph is the owner and operator of Abate-A-Pest. He has been dedicated to controlling pests since 1998. He started Abate-A-Pest in 2004 and has been diligent in keeping current with the latest studies, information, and techniques in pest control management. The more he learns, the more he realizes that going green is truly the only way to go. Ralph firmly believes in taking an environmentally friendly approach to life to help save the Earth for ourselves and the generations to follow.
